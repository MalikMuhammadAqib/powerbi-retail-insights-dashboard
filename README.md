# 🛍 Superstore Sales Analysis | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **Superstore retail dataset** using **Power BI** to uncover key insights about sales performance, profitability, regional trends, and customer segments.

The goal of this analysis is to help business stakeholders understand performance patterns and make **data-driven decisions** to improve profitability and growth.

---

# 📂 Dataset

The dataset used for this project is included in this repository.

File included:

- `superstore_sales.csv`

The dataset contains the following information:

- Order ID
- Order Date
- Product Category
- Product Name
- Sales
- Profit
- Customer Segment
- Region

This dataset was cleaned and prepared using **Power Query in Power BI** before building the dashboard.

# 🔍 Key Findings

## 1️⃣ Category Analysis

- **Furniture** generated **$451,508 in sales** but only **$10,006 in profit**, resulting in a very low **2.2% profit margin**.
- **Technology** produced the **highest profit ($90,458)** with a strong **19.2% margin**.
- **Office Supplies** generated the **highest sales volume** at **$643,707**.

**Insight:** High sales do not necessarily translate into high profitability.

---

## 2️⃣ Regional Analysis

- **West region** is the best performer with **$522,441 in sales** and **12.9% profit margin**.
- **Central region** shows weaker performance with only **8.0% margin** despite strong sales.
- **South region** has the **lowest sales ($252,121)**.

**Insight:** Some regions perform significantly better than others, indicating strategic differences.

---

## 3️⃣ Seasonality Analysis

- **November and December** are the strongest sales months due to **holiday season and Black Friday**.
- **January and February** are the weakest months.
- Sales increased significantly from **2019 to 2020**, with some months showing **~200% growth**.

**Insight:** Retail sales show strong seasonal trends.

---

## 4️⃣ Customer Segment Analysis

- **Consumer segment** drives **48% of total revenue ($753,002)**.
- **Home Office segment** has the **highest profit margin (11.9%)**.
- **Corporate segment** generates **$509,743 in sales**.

**Insight:** Each customer segment contributes differently to revenue and profitability.

# 📈 Strategic Recommendations

## 1️⃣ Furniture Strategy

Furniture has an extremely low **2.2% profit margin**.

- Investigate heavy discounting
- Remove low-profit sub-categories
- Optimize pricing strategy

---

## 2️⃣ Regional Focus

The **Central region** is underperforming compared to the **West region**.

- Study successful strategies used in the West
- Implement similar sales approaches in other regions

---

## 3️⃣ Seasonal Planning

Sales spike during the **holiday season**.

- Increase marketing and inventory from **September–December**
- Introduce promotions in **January and February**

---

## 4️⃣ Customer Strategy

- Expand **Corporate customer acquisition** for bulk orders
- Maintain strong service for the **Consumer segment**, which drives nearly half of revenue

---

## 5️⃣ Growth Opportunity

The **South region** and **Home Office segment** show growth potential.

Targeted expansion and marketing could significantly increase overall revenue.
